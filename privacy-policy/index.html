Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how our application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you connect your Instagram or TikTok accounts.

Information We Collect

  • Basic profile details (such as account ID, username) from connected platforms
  • Access tokens required to publish posts on your behalf
  • Content (images, captions, videos) you upload through the App

How We Use Information

The information is used only to provide the App’s features, such as creating, scheduling, and publishing social media posts. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage

All tokens and content are stored securely in our database and are accessible only to provide the App’s functionality.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at youremail@example.com. We will remove all associated records within 30 days.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at youremail@example.com.
